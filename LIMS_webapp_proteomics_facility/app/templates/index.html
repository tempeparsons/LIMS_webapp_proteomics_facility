Welcome to the CIMR Proteomics facility. We provide state-of-the-art LC-MS/MS and LC-MS/MS/MS analysis on our Fusion Lumos and QExactive Orbitraps. We perform protein profiling, intact mass-analysis and targeted proteomics for Cambridge University, external academic institutes and commercial partners. We have the capacity for start-to-finish sample processing, including running gels, digesting proteins, labelling and fractionating peptide samples. Our highly experienced staff support facility users with experimental design, interpreting results, statistical analyses and manuscript preparation.
