Protein Discovery at CIMR


We offer a wide range of quantitative discovery Proteomics workflows including SILAC, TMT and label free quantitation (LFQ) which can be acquired in a data dependent (DDA) or data independent (DIA) fashion. Data can be processed in MaxQuant, DIA-NN, Proteome Discoverer or PEAKS and we offer support with data interpretation and presentation. We have experience with sub-cellular Proteomics such as Plasma-Membrane Profiling and LOPIT as well as PTM enrichment strategies.
